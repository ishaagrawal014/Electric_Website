FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to what homeowners usually ask before booking. Don't see yours? Call (951) 555-0182.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — CA C-10 License #948213, and we carry full liability and workers' comp coverage.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes, on-site quotes are free with no obligation. Phone estimates are available for straightforward jobs.

How fast can you schedule a job?

Most non-emergency jobs are scheduled within a week. Emergency calls are handled same-day, 24/7.

Do you pull permits?

Yes, on every job that requires one — it's included in your quote, not an add-on fee.

What areas do you serve?

Riverside, Brookfield, Moreno Valley, and Corona, CA. See our Service Areas page for details.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Every installation comes with a 1-year workmanship warranty in addition to manufacturer warranties on parts.
