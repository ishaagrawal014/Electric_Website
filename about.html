About Ironclad Electric

Built by electricians, not a franchise.

Ironclad Electric started in 2011 when our founder, a journeyman electrician, got tired of homeowners being quoted three different prices for the same panel swap. We publish our pricing, pull permits on every job, and send the same two-person crew from first visit to final sign-off.

Licensed & insured

CA C-10 License #948213 · Bonded & Insured. Every electrician on our crew holds a current state license and completes annual code-update training.

Local, not corporate

We're based on 482 Foundry St in Riverside and only serve Riverside County — no call centers, no subcontracted crews.
